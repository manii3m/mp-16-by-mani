Refund and Return Policy


At MP-16 Store, we take utmost pride in providing our customers with high-quality products and exceptional customer service. However, we understand that occasionally a customer may want to return or seek a refund for a purchase. In order to ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience for our valued customers, we have formulated a comprehensive refund and return policy.
Our refund and return policy is designed to protect the interests of our customers while also maintaining the sustainability and integrity of our business. We believe in offering fair and reasonable terms so that our customers feel confident and secure when making a purchase with us.

1. Eligibility for a Refund or Return:

At MP-16 Store, customers are eligible for a refund or return under the following circumstances:

- Damaged or defective products: If a product is delivered to you in a damaged condition or is defective, we will gladly offer a full refund or provide a replacement. Please make sure to notify us within 48 hours of receiving the product and provide photographic evidence of the damage or defect.

- Incorrectly delivered products: In case you receive a product that does not match your original order, we will arrange for the correct item to be delivered to you as soon as possible, free of charge. Alternatively, you may also choose to receive a full refund.

- Dissatisfaction with the product: If you are not fully satisfied with your purchase, we offer a 14-day return window. The product must be returned unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition as you received it. Please note that in such cases, the return shipping cost will be borne by the customer. Once the product is received and inspected, we will issue a refund minus any applicable shipping charges.

2. Process for Requesting a Refund or Return:

To initiate a refund or return request, please contact our customer service team via email or phone within the specified time period mentioned above. Provide relevant details such as order number, product details, and the reason for the refund or return. Our dedicated team will guide you through the process and provide assistance every step of the way.

3. Exceptions to the Refund and Return Policy:

Please note that certain products may not be eligible for a refund or return due to their nature, hygiene reasons, or if explicitly mentioned on the product page. Additionally, any customized or personalized products cannot be returned or refunded unless they are found to be damaged or defective upon arrival.

4. Timeframe for Refunds:

Once a refund is approved, we will process it within 7-10 business days. The actual time it takes for the refund to reflect in your account may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.


We hope this refund and return policy brings transparency and confidence to your shopping experience at MP-16 Store. We strive to provide the best possible service to our customers, and any feedback regarding our policy is always welcome.



Remember, at MP-16 Store, your satisfaction is our priority, and we are here to assist you in every possible way to ensure a positive shopping experience.
